A fleuron is a typographic element, or glyph, used either as a punctuation mark or as an ornament for typographic compositions. Fleurons are stylized forms of flowers or leaves; the term derives from the Old French word \emph{floron} for flower.\footnote{\url{http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fleuron\_(typography)}}
